## Ingredients

* 1 tsp salt
* 2 tsp garlic powder
* 2 ½ tsp paprika
* 1 tsp ground black pepper
* 1 tsp onion powder
* 1 ¼ tsp dried oregano
* 1 ¼ tsp dried thyme
* ½ tsp red pepper flakes
* 1 tsp cayenne pepper(optional)
* ½ tsp fennel seeds(optional)
